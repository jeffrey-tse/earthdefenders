Who Are We

Earth Defenders is a Canada-based non-profit organization that not only informs and educates people about the global warming issue, but also engages people to participate in the campaign.

What We Do

  • Educate people about global warming
  • Advise people on how to make change
  • Motivate people to take action
  • Plant trees and organize volunteer works
  • Unite people towards the fight against climate change

Become an Earth Defender

  • Get the latest news on global warming
  • Attend various events and meetings
  • Connect with others around the world
  • Participate in volunteer workshops
  • Help defend our planet!

Volunteer Opportunities

  • We offer various volunteer opportunities for Earth Defenders.

  • Outdoor opportunities: plant trees, collect seeds, nurse saplings, sustainable produce research & development work.

  • Indoor opportunities: event planning, social media, public relations, photography, graphic design, data management, etc.

Events & Workshops

  • Online workshops are held for Earth Defenders to obtain the latest news and innovative solutions.

  • We also organize indoor and outdoor events so that members can make connections with each other and become more actively involved.
