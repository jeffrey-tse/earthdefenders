Small changes,
big differences.

  • Clothing

    • Choose products made with eco-friendly materials
    • Avoid synthetic fabric/cotton and substitute with linen/silk ones
    • Recycle old clothes - make donations
    • Buy second-hand clothes instead of new ones (ex. at thrift stores!)

  • Diet

    • Purchase local ingredients, seasonally grown, organic food
    • Eat more vegetables and less meat and dairy products
    • Do not waste food
    • Make your own meals and consider eating out less

  • Living

    • Choose reusable products
    • Avoid goods with lots of packaging
    • Unplug devices that are not in use
    • Consume resources and energy wisely, do not waste

  • Travelling

    • Drive less and walk more
    • Switch to electric vehicles
    • Bike or take public transportation
    • Purchase carbon offsets
    • Carpool

  • Make sure to spread the word!

    • Share these tips with friends and family
    • Start to make small changes in daily life
    • Join our social media campaign
    • Become an Earth Defender!
